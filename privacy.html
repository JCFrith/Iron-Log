Iron Log

Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

The short version

Iron Log stores your account details and training data so your history follows you between devices, and shares your training data with your coach so they can coach you. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, and we don't use third-party analytics or trackers. You can export or delete everything.

What we collect

  • Account information — email address, name, and a hashed password (we never see or store your password in readable form).
  • Training data — workouts you log: exercises, weights, reps, session and exercise notes, deload history, activity sessions (e.g. yoga or cardio), and your assigned training program.
  • Body metrics — bodyweight and measurements, if you choose to log them.
  • Preferences — settings such as your color theme.
  • WHOOP data (optional) — if you choose to connect a WHOOP account, we receive the data you authorize (such as recovery, sleep, and profile information) to display it in the app and inform your training. We never receive your WHOOP password. You can disconnect WHOOP at any time, which stops all further access.

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or anything from your device beyond what's listed above.

How your data is used

  • To run the app: sync your history across devices, calculate training targets, and show your progress analytics.
  • To enable coaching: your coach on Iron Log can view your training data — sessions, notes, body metrics, program, and (if connected) recovery summaries — and can leave feedback on your sessions. This visibility is the point of the product; if you do not want a coach to see your training data, do not use a coached account.
  • To respond if you contact us for support.

We do not sell or rent your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for advertising of any kind.

Where it lives

Data is stored with Supabase, our database and authentication provider, and transmitted over HTTPS. Access is enforced with row-level security: you can access your own records, and coach accounts can additionally read their athletes' training records. A copy of your recent data is also cached on your own device so the app works with a weak gym signal.

Your controls

  • Export — download your complete training data as JSON anytime from the System panel in the app.
  • Delete — email us at jcfrith@gmail.com from your account email and we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.
  • Disconnect WHOOP — revoke Iron Log's access anytime from the app or from your WHOOP account settings.
  • Local-only mode — you can use the app without an account; data then stays on your device and is never uploaded.

Health disclaimer

Iron Log records fitness data and displays training targets. It is not a medical device and provides no medical advice. Consult a qualified professional before beginning or changing an exercise program.

Children

Iron Log is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the effective date above and note the change in the app. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.
